A Little about Myself


I am a Data Science Enthusiast, passionate about Data Analytics, Machine Learning & Deep Learning. I consider myself perceptive, results driven and team player. Proficient in both Python & R. I have a diverse set of skills & experiences in different python frameworks & libraries that includes Numpy, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Pytorch, Tensorflow, Plotly, Dash and Flask.
In case of R, I have work experience with ggplot2, dplyr, tidyr and tibble.

My dissertation was focused on Natural Language Processing titled as Personality Detection from Text using Convolutional Neural Network.
I am passionate about making positive impact to the society through my knowledge. As for my personal life, I like to travel, and I love football.
